Know Mateus Henrique

I'm a web and mobile developer passionate about building digital experiences that make an impact. Based in Rio de Janeiro, started the journey in computing in 2019 and never stopped ever since.
With a strong foundation in software development and a relentless drive to improve, I turn ideas into functional and high-performing applications.

A very long trip to get here.

Started my carreer with computing at CEFET/RJ, the best vocational high school of Rio de Janeiro. During my studies, I focused on skills I considered fundamental, earning international certifications like CCNA (Cisco network certification) and PCAP (Python)

Finishing my high-school, I landed my first internship in web development at CEFET/RJ. In the meanwhile, I also started developing local websites in my area. Finished the internship, went to Rede D'or developing and supporting operations. There, I learned various technologies.

In 2024, I was hired as a Mobile Developer by De Olho No Pé, where I still work today.

Seeing this diverse joruney, I decided to bring together a group of trustful developers and founded 01bit.tech, a team focused on covering all stages of software development, from design to implementation.
